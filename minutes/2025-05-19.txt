ST. LOUIS COUNTY LIBRARY DISTRICT
SPECIAL MEETING OF THE BOARD
VIRTUAL HELD VIA ZOOM
MONDAY, MAY 19, 2025
The St. Louis County Library did not hold an in-person public Board Meeting on this day. This Special Meeting of the Board of Trustees was held virtually via Zoom on Monday, May 19, 2025, at 3:30 p.m. The agenda had been posted in all branches of the Library District at least 24 hours before the meeting.

Motion by Joan Barry, second by Lynn Beckwith.
