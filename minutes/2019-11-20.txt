FLORISSANT VALLEY BRANCH
195 NEW FLORISSANT ROAD S.
MONDAY, NOVEMBER 20, 2019
2:20 p.m.
The special meeting of the Board of the St. Louis County Library District was held at the Florissant Valley Branch, 195 New Florissant Road S., St. Louis, MO 63031 on Monday, November 20, 2019.   The agenda was posted in all branches of the Library District at least 24 hours before the meeting.
ATTENDANCE:
Members present:
Dr. Lynn Beckwith, President
Joan Barry, Member
Laura Horwitz, Member
Ted Sanditz, Member
Johnny Wang, Member
ADMINISTRATORS AND GUESTS ATTENDING:
Kristen Sorth, Library Director; Eric Button, Deputy Director, Barbara Turkington, Assistant Director, Development; Kris Mooney, Assistant Director, Administration; Gib Van Cleve, Assistant Director, ITS, Steve Hunter, Assistant Director, Operations, Lisa Stump, Lashly and Baer and Erin Achenbach, St. Louis Call Newspapers.
OTHER ATTENDING:
Dan Lucas and Sandra Williams.
Dr. Beckwith called the meeting to order and requested each Board member introduce themselves. Members introduced themselves in the following order, Dr. Lynn Beckwith, Joan Barry, Laura Horwitz, Ted Sanditz and Johnny Wang.
Dr. Beckwith said he was looking forward to working with all of them.
A motion to adjourn open meeting and to convene Executive Session to discuss legal matters (Section 610.021(1)) was made by Joan Barry and seconded by Johnny Wang.
Aye: Lynn Beckwith, Ted Sanditz, Laura Horwitz, Joan Barry, Johnny Wang
EXECUTIVE SESSION
Open Session Reconvened at 3:55 p.m.
Following executive session Dr. Beckwith explained the roles of Board members and that a vote would be held on Monday, Nov. 25, 2019 for officer positions.
Kristen Sorth gave an overview of the resource book given to each Board Member which included Bylaws and Ethics, State Library Statutes, Strategic Plan, Organizational Chart, Pension Bylaws, Foundation Board and ByLaws and Boarddocs.
Kris Mooney, Assistant Director, Administration
Kris Mooney discussed the Library’s budgets, budgeting process, Treasurer’s Report and procurement process.  He also spoke about the yearly audit.
Sandra Williams, Executive Assistant
Sandra Williams gave a brief training for the BoardDocs program.
ADJOURNMENT
A motion was made to adjourn the meeting at 4:29 p.m. All voted in approval (Wang/Horwitz).
Respectfully submitted,
