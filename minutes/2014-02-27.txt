ST. LOUIS COUNTY LIBRARY DISTRICT
	MEETING OF THE BOARD
	HEADQUARTERS BRANCH
	THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 27, 2014
	The monthly meeting of the Board of the St. Louis County Library District was held at
	Headquarters, 1640 S. Lindbergh Blvd. St. Louis, MO 63131 on Thursday, February 27, 2014
	at 3:30 p.m. The agenda had been posted in all branches of the Library District at least 24 hours
	before the meeting.
	Attendance:
	Library Board Members all present via telephone:
	 Dr. Lynn Beckwith, President
	 Edith Cunnane, Vice President
	 Elena Kenyon, Treasurer
	 Dr. Chingling Tai, Secretary
	 Steve Sachs, Member
	Administrators and Guests Attending:
	Kristen Sorth, Ed Clark, Eric Button and Lisa Stump (via telephone).
	Dr. Beckwith called the meeting to order with a roll call vote.  All were present.
	New Business
	Carpet
	The Library Board approved the recommendation for Stephens Floor Covering Co. for the purchase and installation of carpeting at Headquarters in the administrative area, business office, and the south entrance area. Funds for this work will be deducted from the 2014 Building Repair budget. (Cunnane/Kenyon).   Discussion was held.  Ayes: Dr. Beckwith; Mrs. Cunnane; Mrs. Kenyon, Dr. Tai, and Mr. Sachs.
	Adjourn
	Mr. Sachs made a motion to adjourn.  Mrs. Cunnane seconded.  All approved by voice vote.  Meeting adjourned at 3:37 p.m.
